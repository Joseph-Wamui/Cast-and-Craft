Tender Management & EGP Portal Support

Structured tender management from cost preparation to secure electronic submission

Cast & Craft Ventures offers a structured three-tier tender management service designed to support clients from cost preparation to secure electronic submission via the EGP Portal. Our comprehensive approach ensures compliance, accuracy, and successful bid submissions.

Our 3-Tier Service Model

Comprehensive support at every stage of the tender process

Tier 1: BOQ Preparation & Rate Analysis

Professional bill of quantities development with competitive rate analysis to ensure your bid is both accurate and competitive.

  • Review tender documents
  • Competitive rate analysis
  • BOQ completion
  • Internal QS validation
  • Client approval before submission

Tier 2: EGP Portal Onboarding & Compliance Setup

Complete setup and verification to ensure your organization is fully compliant and ready for electronic submissions.

  • EGP registration & profile setup
  • Document verification
  • Compliance checklist validation
  • Secure handling of portal credentials
  • Ensuring statutory readiness

Tier 3: Tender Compilation & Secure Submission

Final compilation, verification, and secure electronic submission with complete documentation and proof of submission.

  • Compilation of technical & financial documentation
  • Integration of approved BOQ
  • Final compliance verification
  • Client GO/NO-GO confirmation
  • Secure upload to EGP portal
  • Submission acknowledgment & proof
