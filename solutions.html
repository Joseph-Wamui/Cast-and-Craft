Our Delivery Plan

A structured 5-phase approach ensuring successful project outcome

We employ a rigorous project delivery methodology that prioritizes stakeholder alignment, risk management, and quality control from initiation through to closure.

A

Initiation

  • Review contract documents
  • Develop Project Charter & Execution Plan
  • Identify stakeholders and establish communication protocols
B

Planning

  • Develop POW (clearly marked the critical path & milestones)
  • Establish cost baseline & budget framework
  • Prepare Quality, Risk & HSE management plans
  • Develop procurement & communication plans
C

Execution

  • Mobilize contractors and resources
  • Supervise construction activities and verify quality
  • Monitor progress against schedule and budget
  • Coordinate logistics, procurement, and stakeholder engagement
D

Monitoring & Control

  • Weekly and monthly progress reporting
  • Earned Value Analysis for cost/schedule performance
  • Risk register updates and mitigation tracking
  • Quality audits and HSE inspections
E

Closure

  • Snagging and defect rectification
  • Commissioning and handover documentation
  • Final account settlement and lessons learned workshop
  • Post-project review and performance benchmarking
